Sierpinski Triangle & Mandelbrot Set

Sierpinski Triangle
Shape colors are generated based on a hash of the shape's position and size, added to the frameCount,
then plugged into different sine/cosine functions to generate a yellow/blue color gradient.
Press W to increase the number of iterations that are used to generate the fractal.
S will decrease the number of iterations.
Press up arrow to increase the number of sides of each polygon. This also increases the number that spawn at each iteration.
down arrow will decrease both of these variables.
Press A to spread apart the shapes. This is EXTREMELY important for the square fractal, otherwise it will just form a large square!
D will attract the shapes.
Mandelbrot Set
Press T to increase the resolution of the image. Higher resolution images will take exponentially longer to render.
G decreases the resolution.
Press WASD to move the fractal around the screen.
Press up arrow to zoom in and down arrow to zoom out.
Press right arrow to increase the number of iterations used to generate the fractal. (Makes it more intricate)
left arrow will decrease the number of iterations.
